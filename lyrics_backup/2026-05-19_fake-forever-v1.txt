[Hook 1]
Tere naina da nasha chadhda ae every night
Main tere piche aunda haan out of the light
Ni main ho gaya deewana losing my control

[Hook 2]
Tera nakhra vi denda ae dangerous vibe
Tainu dekh ke main bhull gaya saara divide

[Verse 1]
Labbda main tainu in the midnight hour
Tera husn karda ae mainu overpower
Teri ikk jhalak dendi ae mainu taan freeze
Meri jind udd di ae blowing the breeze
Thandi hawa vich udde teri dark hair
Tera roop chadh gaya in the air
Akhiyan 'ch takkda main falling so deep
Tere khayalan vich losing my sleep

[Pre-Chorus]
Teri mehak vich bhull gaya main saara jahan
Bullan utte naam tera giving me the spark
Udd gaye ne hosh mere ho gaya haan fida
Chann di roshni vich piercing the dark

[Chorus]
Ho gaya haan deewana main forever and ever
Chhad ke na jaavin mainu sohniye tu never
Tere layi main raahan vich karda haan cross
Ishq de is khel vich no counting the loss
Dil karda ae tainu dewa my love
Aaja mere kol lagdi sent from above
Meri har saah vich chaldi ae tu
Main taan tere piche aunda what can I do

[Verse 2]

Hass ke tu kadd layi ae meri eh rooh

Teri muskaan dendi ae mainu heart attack
Rukda na kade main no stepping back
